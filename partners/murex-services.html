Murex 

As a Murex Global Partner, LiquidHub is helping financial institutions worldwide to accelerate implementation time, improve operation efficiency, and unlock increased value through tailored Murex services.

Our proven engagement models combined with our skills in Murex’s methodologies enable clients to better leverage the platform to improve front, middle, and back office operation capabilities.

As an extension of your IT department, LiquidHub ensures your Murex deployment stays current, while reducing risk, delays, performance issues, and overall disruption to your business.

MUREX SERVICES

LiquidHub provides implementation, version upgrade support, and managed services to financial institutions with Murex worldwide.

Murex Implementation

Want to ensure your Murex implementation is carried out on time and in budget?

Services include:

  • Planning & Management – proven methodology and experience of Murex Green field implementation
  • Integration – analysis, design, mapping and implementation of interfaces up and down stream, market data, reporting and environment support
  • Implementation – dedicated project managers for scoping, phasing, managing, costing & delivery

Murex Upgrade

Do you need support to ensure your deployment stays current without disrupting day to day business?

Services include:

  • Upgrade strategy and process – stable, on-time, and reliable upgrades
  • System Review – best practices, gap analysis and remediation recommendations
  • Performance Management – monitoring, alerting, troubleshooting and reporting

Murex Managed Services

Want to drive increased value from your Murex implementation, improve operation efficiency, but are struggling to find the right skills in-house?

Services include:

  • Application Management – release and environment management
  • Test Management – end to end testing for quality and performance
  • Data visualization – reports & dashboards
  • Process automation
  • 24/7 global support – L1-L3 Murex applications
  • Staff Augmentation – short or long term, onsite or global delivery services
