LiquidHub Appoints Tony Cosentino as Data and Analytics Evangelist

PHILADELPHIA – June 13, 2017 – LiquidHub, a customer engagement company, is pleased to announce the appointment of Tony Cosentino as Data and Analytics Evangelist. Tony will lead business development for LiquidHub’s Data and Analytics Practice, working closely with clients to enable faster, more informed decisions through enhanced data-driven customer insights.

Commenting on the appointment, Rahul Sahgal, Global Head of Data & Analytics Practice at LiquidHub said, “Tony is a recognized industry expert on strategy, analytics and business intelligence and we are excited to welcome him to our team. He brings extensive experience in leading sales and deployment of end-to-end customer analytics initiatives including some of the first global cloud-based analytics and reporting applications. Bringing Tony on to the team will enable us to accelerate our growth plans and expand our reach into new areas.”

“As corporations continue to embrace the value of data-driven strategies, data and analytics remains a critically important growth area for businesses across the country,” noted Cosentino. “LiquidHub’s proven success in providing actionable insights to clients in a variety of industries has enabled them to keep up with the accelerated pace of change in today’s technologically-driven world. I look forward to joining the team as it continues to expand and innovate.”

Tony most recently served as a Senior Industry Analyst on business intelligence, operational intelligence and advanced analytics at a business technology advisory and consulting company. He is also the author of “Into the River” covering the impact of big data, crowd-sourcing, and behavioral analytics on the modern organization.

About LiquidHub
LiquidHub is a customer engagement company that partners with businesses to improve customer experience and drive growth. Headquartered in Philadelphia, with operations in North America, Asia, and Europe, we serve companies globally, helping them solve their most complex challenges through design expertise and technology innovation. Our customer successes are the result of a culture rooted in thought leadership and delivery excellence.
