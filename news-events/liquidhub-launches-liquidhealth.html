LiquidHub Launches LiquidHealth

Digital health pioneer Eveo joins LiquidHub to form new strategic practice

PHILADELPHIA – June 27, 2017 – LiquidHub, a customer engagement company, today announced the launch of LiquidHealth, a new practice aimed at re-imagining engagement in healthcare. LiquidHealth expands LiquidHub’s life sciences and healthcare solutions by combining LiquidHub’s established solutions and capabilities with Eveo’s 17+ years of experience in digital health marketing, to create insights-driven and innovative experiences for patients, providers, and payers. The acquisition expands LiquidHub’s team of digital specialists across the healthcare ecosystem.

LiquidHealth will combine strategic, creative, and digital services with LiquidHub’s rapidly growing data offering for the specialty pharmaceutical industry, LiquidAnalytics. The practice is poised to offer a fresh approach to health marketers, where innovative thinking is combined with measurement at the prescription and patient level in order to deliver ROI on the deepest, most engaging customer experiences.

“It is not just technology or design that is at LiquidHub’s core – it is people,” said Jonathan Brassington, co-founder and CEO of LiquidHub. “We first view patients, providers, and payers as people, and then develop and apply solutions to help our clients better engage with them.”

Olivier Zitoun, founder and CEO of Eveo, commented, “The next frontier in healthcare marketing is engagement. By blending Eveo’s innovative approach to patient and provider engagement with LiquidHub’s real-world insights, we will provide ground-breaking and truly measurable solutions to health brands.” Zitoun created Eveo in 1999, as one of the first digital health agencies. It was ranked by AdAge as the #1 digital health agency in the U.S. for three years in a row and evolved as a full service strategic agency, focused on specialty pharma/biotech, diagnostics, and med devices brands. Eveo won over 200 awards for its creative and innovative work.

LiquidHealth’s offering will include the following capabilities and solutions:

  • Strategic planning
  • Branding & messaging
  • Omni-channel strategy
  • Experience design
  • Immersive engagement (AR/VR)
  • Intelligent experience (AI/ML)
  • Sales force effectiveness
  • Next-gen CRM
  • Social engagement
  • Real-world insights
  • Predictive analytics
