Research – Know your customer

Our research and design professionals use a collection of methods to observe and gain unique insights. We help our clients see and understand how people interact with technology and their overall feelings for the brand – employees, customers, and partners.

As part of our strategy, we regularly conduct original research for our clients in order to gain a better understanding of their audiences and customers, and the mental models that guide behavior and decision making.

In the lab, remotely, in an office, a car, or on the street, our designers, human factor experts, and researchers come together to observe, document, and improve the human experience. Apart from developing questionnaires, guides, and supporting materials, our research services include:

Our team has received training from the United States National Institutes of Health (NIH) and have passed their certification course, "Human Participant Protections: Education for Research Teams.”

We strictly adhere to Article IV of the Human Factors and Ergonomics Society’s Code of Ethics regarding Subject Precautions.
