Strategy – Establishing a strong foundation and a clear path to success

People and their experience are central to all that we do, at every stage. Strategy, to be complete, must consider the level of human (read here empathy understanding) we have for anything we design and build. In addition, any strategy, plan, or roadmap requires deep understanding of the industry, technology, and the expectations of a new clientele demanding everything personalized, and immediately accessible.

Research & Discovery: Centered around our philosophy that design, architecture, and development are greatly improved by a multidisciplinary approach to understanding context, possibilities, and form.
Our capabilities include:

  • Human-Centered Research
  • Ethnography
  • Observational Analysis
  • Requirements Management
  • Journey Mapping
  • Experience (Usability) Testing
  • Structured Innovation
  • Rapid Solution Modeling

THINKFAST Structured Innovation and R&D: Flexible, Customizable, Scalable, and Fast. A unique set of presentations, workshops, immersion sessions, and collaborative activities that get your team focused on innovative business ideas that are insightful, actionable, and financially impactful.
Our services include:

  • Idea Generation Workshops
  • Quarterly Briefings & Lecture Series
  • Opportunity Framing
  • Concept Presentations
  • Concept to Prototype
  • MVP Launch Programs

Experience Management: Memorable experiences require you to design and architect user-centric experiences that strike the right balance between functionality, and delight both onscreen and in physical environments.
Our capabilities include:

  • Experience Strategy Formulation
  • Content Strategy Formulation
  • Interface Design and Validation
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • Design Specification
  • Future-State Road Mapping

Digital Architecture: Digital business models require today’s enterprise technology environments to be rationalized and re-architected. We combine our tried and tested Enterprise Architectures expertise with our integration and continuous innovation capabilities to help you modernize your architecture.
Our services include:

  • Engagement Platforms Strategy
  • Experience Architecture
  • Enterprise Business & Technology Architecture
  • Application Portfolio Optimization
  • Technology Strategy & Road-mapping
  • Digital Integration Architecture
