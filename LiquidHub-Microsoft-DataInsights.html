Tuesday, June 13
10:00am - 10:20am

Getting Business Value from Social Media and Unstructured Data – The Power of Power BI

Social media is changing the way organizations engage with customers, manage relationships, and innovate products and services.  Social media has become an important part of business strategy.

In this presentation, we will share how to leverage social and unstructured data to gain intelligence – identify trends, discover topics and relationships, predict behavior, and get actionable insights across all social media and other online channels. We will explore the winning fusion of social media analytics and Power BI, sharing real world examples, use cases, and lessons learned.

Monday, June 12
11:00am - 11:20am

Visualize the Internet of Things – The Power of Power BI

In today’s connected world, IoT contributes significantly to the global flow of data creating both challenges and opportunities to make insight driven decisions.  Power BI with the Cortana Intelligence Suite provides the power to visualize IoT data as it streams-in.

In this session, we will share best practices and real world examples of how Power BI and Azure Cloud infrastructure can be used to unlock the promise of the SMART building sector. We will showcase how real-time analytics can fill the information gap to optimize building operations, improve energy efficiency management and provide visibility, control and automation to building systems.
