The industry of grocery shopping is shifting at a rapid pace. Shoppers journeys are different than they were ten, five, even two years ago.

Shoppers are no longer loyal to one supermarket. In fact, the Food Market Institute found that last year, only 49% of shoppers chose a supermarket as their primary grocery store. The average shopper is utilizing anywhere from five to seven retail channels altogether, and two or three channels regularly to meet his or her grocery needs.

In this rapidly changing market, grocers need to create a digital vision, built on shopper research, that guides how they digitally engage with shoppers.

We know that the store is at the heart of your business, but we also believe that digital offers an increasing number of touchpoints — each of which is an opportunity to amplify relationships and reach shoppers where they live, work and play.

We help grocers expand digital channels by:

  • Better understanding the shopper and their experiences interacting with your brand across the entire journey
  • Designing digital engagement tools that meet the needs of your shoppers both in store and out of the store
  • Building working prototypes to test tools in the market, quickly
  • Implementing digital tools that seamlessly integrate with your current technology
