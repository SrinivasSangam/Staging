Enterprise Reporting Factory for an Automobile Manufacturer

A leading Automobile Manufacturer built a large enterprise wise data warehouse solution by bringing numerous business function data into a centralized database for enterprise reporting and business intelligence. They decided to make OBIEE and Qlik as the de-facto platforms for all reporting purposes and also developed canned reports for each business user. The business experienced a constant need to create new reports and received regular requests for enhancements from users, with faster turn around time and less cost.

LiquidHub engaged to provide a global delivery model (onshore and offshore resources) for reporting needs of the client. We assisted to deliver an onshore lead and an offshore team for report development and enhancements. Multiple teams helped the client to develop, test, and provide finished reports for user testing and subsequent production deployment.

LiquidHub was able to meet the demand and need for a large number of reports that were being requested by the users on a regular basis. The onshore lead gathered requirements and provided them to the offshore team which in turn developed, tested, and delivered the final reports. The client saved hundreds of thousands of dollars by using a team of offshore developers.
