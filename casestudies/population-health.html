Population Health

A leader in the healthcare industry, the client aims to provide an extraordinary healthcare experience for members and significant cost savings for health plans. The client wanted to develop clinical solutions that would include 24/7 in-home and phone support for all patients.

They required a solution that would include:

  • HRA
  • Wellness, Care Management, Chronic Conditions Post discharge, ER/Urgent care diversion
  • Care coordination
  • Gap Closure
  • SNF Visits
  • Clinical Rounds
  • Biometrics
  • Health Fairs

LiquidHub designed and built the payer and member portals that enabled users to:

  • Easily schedule visits
  • View medical history
  • Update their profile or medical history
  • View care plans
  • View labs - get condition specific data
  • Generate reports

We enabled a comprehensive and integrated suite of Health & Wellness Services for the client which were technology enabled. These services helped to monitor the health of the population, administer medical care when needed and ensured, on an ongoing basis, the maintenance of the health of the population.

LiquidHub’s solution helped the client to

  • Contain and reduce healthcare costs while ensuring simple convenient access
  • Deploy programs that drive improvements in member health and wellness
  • Provide convenient care to members where and when it’s needed
  • Gain visibility into health trends, use of services, regional variances and overall population health
  • Avoid un-necessary costs and utilization of services including admissions and ER visits
  • Ensure high quality convenient healthcare experience for members
